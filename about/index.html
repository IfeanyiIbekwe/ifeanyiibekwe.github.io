About Me

Digital marketing leader with a strong focus on the luxury hospitality and F&B sectors, bringing a proven track record of crafting transformative strategies that drive brand growth and revenue. Currently, I lead digital marketing for all F&B businesses across Madinat Jumeirah Resort, strategically positioning dining venues at Jumeirah Al Qasr, Mina A'Salam, and Al Naseem. Over the past year, my data-driven campaigns have achieved a 20% increase in reservations, set new benchmarks for customer retention, and driven exceptional ROI.

Among my key accomplishments, I successfully launched the opening campaign for Cala Vista at Mina A’Salam, creating a multi-funnel digital approach that exceeded reservation targets. At FIVE Hotels and Resorts, I developed and executed the launch for Soul Street, establishing it as a vibrant culinary destination. Recently, I led the “Balloons at the Palace” tactical campaign, a special activation at Jumeirah Al Qasr, which captured guest engagement through an immersive guest journey and unique culinary offerings.

In addition, I co-founded The Business of Preloved Fashion (BOPF), a luxury resale platform with a mission to democratize high-end fashion. Since its inception, BOPF has generated 5M AED in revenue, underscoring my ability to drive growth across both corporate and entrepreneurial environments.
